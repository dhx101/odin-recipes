How to Make Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Lasagna Ingredients

The Allrecipes community adores this lasagna because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though. these are the ingredients you wi'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

How to Make Lasagna Step-by-step

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna

  1. Make the meat souce
  2. Cooke the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

How to Layer Lasagna

The detailed layering instructions can be found in the recipe below, but this is the order you'll follow

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with remaining Permesan

How to cook Lasagna

In order to cook the lasagna you should use an oven and it should take about 50 mins to cook.

First you should preheat your oven to 375 degrees F. or 190 degrees C.Cover it with foil for the first 25 mins, then let it cook undercovered for the final 25 mins. Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for 15 minutes before you cut into it.
